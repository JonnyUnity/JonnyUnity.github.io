paragraph1: <p>I took part in Narrative Imperative Game Jam \#1 in a team of four. The jam lasted for two weeks and the theme was "This is bigger than you".</p><p>I was the only developer in the team and also acted as project manager/producer arranging catch-up calls and handling assets from art, narrative and audio.</p>

img2: /officesecrets/Bob.png
img2-alt: A visit from Bob

paragraph2: <p>We knew we wanted to make a game involving some kind of conspiracy and with narrative being the focus of the jam I was keen for your choices to matter, ideally utilising the "person will remember this" concept from Telltale games. Whilst we didn't end up doing that I did program a dialogue system that would branch based on previous responses made.</p>

img3: /officesecrets/TheBoss.png
